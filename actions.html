Make your own Actions

Step 1

Select an activity

Choose from a growing list of activities. Tommy is learning more and you can expand what Tommy can do for you via the Add-on Store, or you can build your own.

Step 2

What triggers Tommy?

Tommy is on the lookout 24/7. Tell Tommy what “Trigger” event he should pay attention to and he'll jump into action. The list of Triggers is constantly growing but in general they relate to user activity, time and the odd random thing like weather.

Step 3

What are the rules?

Tommy is a good boy. He doesn't just bark when the door is knocked. Give him some rules and he'll make sure all the boxes are ticked first. Rules can focus Triggers to specific users, during specific time period, even to the weather forecasts.

Step 4

Confirm activity details

Tommy now knows what activity you want to achieve and when to do it, but he needs some specifics, like what should be in the message or email. This step looks different depending on the activity you've chosen though for the most part it is a simple form for you to specify or confirm what is recommended.

Step 5

Let Tommy take it from here.

Always be on the lookout for these repetitive things that Tommy can handle for you.
