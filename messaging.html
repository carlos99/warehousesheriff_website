Open Chats

Create an Open Chat to communicate openly and keep everyone in your team in the loop.

Make an Open Chat for a project, a department, anything.

Transparent discussions

Open Chats allow non-participants to read messages and elect to participate themselves.

Create semi-open chats

Want to make an Open Chat visible for one Department? Easy. You can define who has open access based on Team Status, Location, Role or a combination of each.

Only those engaged in an Open Chat will receive push notifications of new unread messages. Users may also opt-out of receiving further notifications.
