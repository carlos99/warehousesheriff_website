Platform / Enterprise

Enterprise Solution

Our comprehensive team management offering

Tommy Enterprise delivers customised, scalable team messaging and activity automation solutions for companies leveraging cloud efficiency.

We provide tailored team messaging available across all popular mobile operating systems,expandable platform to provide flexibility of experience for your team, assistance in solution design, and add-on developer services.

The result is a better workforce management system, operational faster and more affordably than by traditional means.

  • Team Messaging

  • Automated Actions

  • Technology Platform

  • Premium Service

Team Messaging

Ideal for group conversations where sensitive information maybe exchanged.

Get your teams operating in real-time. The average response window to an email is 4-6hours whereas the average response window for a message is 15-60 minutes.

Create Open Chats where anyone permitted may read without participating. Its a great way to keep everyone in the loop.

Include operational notifications from third party software within messaging so there is no need to unnecessary jump between applications for visibility.

Take control of instant messaging, which is most likely already happening in an ad-hoc fashion often violating security and compliance policy.

Delight your HR department by introducing team messaging into the on-boarding procedure.

Automated Actions

Ideal for group conversations where sensitive information maybe exchanged.

Discover repetitive tasks and implement Actions to automatically manage reoccurrence, freeing up your teams time while reducing possibility of human error.

The pool of pre-defined Actions is constantly growing so every day there are new opportunities for greater efficiency in your company.

Count the dollars and the time saved. Audit log tracks everything Tommy Action's have performed on your behalf giving you insight into the hours saved on an activity level.

(Note: Your Team members can set their own personal Actions, which only they have visibility)

Technology Platform

Purposely built to enable developers in enterprise companies to connect operational software as private add-ons and expand accessibility to the BYOD team member.

Confidently expand what can be achieved within Tommy, enhancing your team communication and mobile efficiency without the worries of maintaining infrastructure.

Compliance and Auditing

Tommy collects all business activity performed in real-time. We track when an end useropens an add-on, when end user downloads a document, and more. Tommy's Platform collects this data and makes it available in real-time.

Events data can also be uploadedto your existing risk management system, e.g., Splunk.

Scalable and continuous availability

Tommy's Infrastructure is running across multiple cloud data centres for maximum uptime and our team of in-house developers are constantly working to eliminate any possible single points of failure that maybe possible.

Cloud and Private Cloud

Tommy is SaaS running on Amazon AWS. This is multi-tenancy and what we generally refer to as the Cloud.

If your enterprise has requires single-tenant (perhaps bare-metal)infrastructure or requires HIPAA... Lets talk.

Data Center and Application Security

Keeping your company's data secure is a top priority of Tommy's. Our goal is to providea secure environment, while also being mindful of application performance and the overall user experience
